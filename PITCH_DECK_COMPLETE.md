# CALY v3.0 - COMPLETE PRODUCT PITCH DECK

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Caly** is a **production-ready, AI-powered voice agent platform** for multi-sector e-commerce customer support. It handles inbound calls in Hindi/Hinglish, executes complex agentic tasks (order lookup, cancellations, returns), and delivers real-time performance analytics—all managed through an intuitive supervisor dashboard.
